
# LA Salsa On1 Turn Patterns (Detailed)

This document lists **54 popular LA Salsa On1 patterns**, grouped by **Beginner**, **Intermediate**, **Advanced**, and **Cuban Adapted** levels. Includes timing (where certain), description, rotation, entry, and exit details.

## Beginner Level

### 1. Basic Step
- **Timing**: 1-2-3, 5-6-7
- **Description**: Forward/backward basic on the slot.
- **Rotation**: None
- **Entry**: Closed or open hold
- **Exit**: Neutral

### 2. Crossbody Lead (CBL)
- **Timing**: 1-2-3 (leader opens), 5-6-7 (follower crosses)
- **Description**: Follower crosses to leader's left; leader steps out of the slot.
- **Rotation**: None
- **Entry**: Closed or open hold
- **Exit**: Open hold

### 3. Right Turn (Follower)
- **Timing**: Prep on 1-2; turn on 3 or 5-6-7
- **Description**: Follower performs a clockwise turn on the slot.
- **Rotation**: CW
- **Entry**: LH (leader) to RH (follower)
- **Exit**: Open hold

### 4. Left Turn (Follower)
- **Timing**: Prep on 1-2; turn on 3 or 5-6-7
- **Description**: Follower performs a counter-clockwise turn on the slot.
- **Rotation**: CCW
- **Entry**: LH (leader) to RH (follower)
- **Exit**: Open hold

### 5. Inside Turn
- **Timing**: 1-2-3: Leader opens slot, 5 : Prep on 5, 6-7 Follower turns CCW
- **Description**: Follower rotates inward during CBL.
- **Rotation**: CCW
- **Entry**: LH (leader) to RH (follower)
- **Exit**: Open hold

### 6. Outside Turn
- **Timing**:
  - 3: prep 
  - 5-6-7: Follower turns CW
- **Description**: Follower rotates outward during CBL.
- **Rotation**: CW
- **Entry**: LH (leader) to RH (follower)
- **Exit**: Open hold

### 7. New York Walk / Paseala
- **Timing**: Walk on 5-6-7
- **Description**: Follower walks forward past leader with styling.
- **Rotation**: None
- **Entry**: Open hold
- **Exit**: Open hold

### 8. Check Turn
- **Timing**:
  - 1-2-3: Check on 3
  - 5-6-7: Reverse turn
- **Description**: Follower checks and reverses direction.
- **Rotation**: CW or CCW depending on variation
- **Entry**: Open hold
- **Exit**: Open hold

### 9. Change of Place
- **Timing**: 1-2-3, 5-6-7
- **Description**: Partners swap positions along the slot.
- **Rotation**: None
- **Entry**: Open hold
- **Exit**: Open hold

### 10. Reverse Crossbody Lead
- **Timing**: 1-2-3, 5-6-7
- **Description**: Follower crosses behind leader instead of in front.
- **Rotation**: None
- **Entry**: Open hold
- **Exit**: Open hold

### 11. Leader's Right Turn
- **Timing**: Prep on 1-2-3; turn on 5-6-7
- **Description**: Leader spins clockwise during or after CBL.
- **Rotation**: CW
- **Entry**: Open hold
- **Exit**: Open hold

### 12. Leader's Left Turn
- **Timing**: Prep on 1-2-3; turn on 5-6-7
- **Description**: Leader spins counter-clockwise during or after CBL.
- **Rotation**: CCW
- **Entry**: Open hold
- **Exit**: Open hold

## Intermediate Level

### 13. Hammerlock
- **Timing**: Inside turn on 5-6-7
- **Description**: Follower's arm ends behind their back (usually right arm in LA basics).
- **Rotation**: CCW
- **Entry**: Inside turn from LH (leader) to RH (follower)
- **Exit**: Unwrap via CBL

### 14. Sweetheart (Wrap)
- **Timing**: Wrap on 5-6-7
- **Description**: Follower's arms wrapped in front; leader holds both hands.
- **Rotation**: None
- **Entry**: Double hand hold
- **Exit**: Unwrap via CBL

### 15. Sombrero
- **Timing**: Crossed arms pass on 5-6-7
- **Description**: Both dancers pass under arms like a hat.
- **Rotation**: None
- **Entry**: Crossed hold (LH on top)
- **Exit**: Both hands behind shoulders

### 16. Cape
- **Timing**: 5-6-7
- **Description**: Similar to Sombrero but one arm draped like a cape.
- **Rotation**: None
- **Entry**: Crossed hold (LH on top)
- **Exit**: Open hold

### 17. Copa

- **Exit**: Open hold

### 19. Copa Outside Turn
